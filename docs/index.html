Digital Garden

Roji.py

My hobbyist attempt at a static digital garden generator for my notes.

It aims to be small and simple.

A folder of markdown files are processed into pages and topics.

Pages are linked through wikilink style [[brackets ]].

Topics are listed with the YAML of the markdown files, each topic has its own page, and can be linked to with {topic}.

Why?

Most static site generators concentrate on the blog format. Frustrated, I made my own, inspired by this article, so that I could transform academic and personal notes into a simple website.
